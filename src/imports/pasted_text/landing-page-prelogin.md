━━━ COMPETITIVE BRIEF ━━━
This page must feel decisively better than Atom Learning, Bond 11+, and
11plusonlinetesting.co.uk. Those sites share these weaknesses:
  — Generic SaaS hero sections with no emotional resonance
  — Trust signals that feel like boilerplate (Trustpilot badges, raw numbers)
  — No sense of a child's actual journey or transformation
  — Forgettable visual identity — no ownable brand moment
This page fixes all four. The emotional centre is child transformation:
a parent watching their child move from anxious to confident.

━━━ DESIGN DIRECTION NOTES ━━━
— This is a BRANDED LEARNING PORTAL, not a SaaS product page.
  Every design decision should reinforce: "this place was built for
  your child's 11+ journey" — not "this is another edtech subscription."
— The logo/wordmark placeholder must look like a real brand slot.
  Use a rounded rectangle with a dashed border labelled "[Your Logo]"
  and "[Platform Name]" in a ghost text style — clearly a placeholder
  but proportioned and positioned as if a real brand lives there.
— The hero right column is the BRAND HERO IMAGE — invest design
  effort here. It is the single most important visual on the page.
  It should be distinctive enough that a parent recognises this portal
  from a thumbnail.
— Testimonials MUST include named grammar schools. That specificity
  is what makes them believable — generic testimonials are ignored.
— The transformation arc (anxious → confident) must be felt in the
  copy, the illustrations, and the testimonials — not just stated once.
